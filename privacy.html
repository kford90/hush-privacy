Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

HUSH is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the HUSH mobile application. By using HUSH, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Account details: work name, username, email address, password
  • Profile information: city, state, job role, bio, social handles, profile photos
  • Financial data you enter: shift earnings, tips, tip-outs, monthly goals (stored privately — never shared)
  • Client book entries: contact names, notes, tip history (stored privately)
  • Community content: live check-ins, posts, replies, and direct messages
  • Photos you upload to your profile or venue album

Information collected automatically:

  • Device push notification token (used only to send you alerts you have opted into)
  • Approximate location (city/state level only, used to show relevant community content)
  • Usage activity within the app (e.g., which tabs you visit)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the HUSH app and its features
  • To display your profile and community activity to other HUSH members
  • To send push notifications you have opted into (new followers, messages, live check-ins)
  • To process your subscription through RevenueCat
  • To respond to support requests sent to gethush.app@gmail.com
  • To review reported content and enforce our Community Guidelines

3. What We Never Share

Your earnings, tips, shift history, client book, and financial goals are completely private. They are never displayed to other users, never sold, and never shared with third parties for any purpose.

4. Third-Party Services

HUSH uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — database hosting and authentication
  • Expo / Expo Notifications — app delivery platform and push notifications
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Google Places API — venue search

We do not sell your data to any of these providers or any other third party.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase secure servers with row-level security policies that ensure each user can only access their own private data. We use industry-standard practices to protect your information.

6. Community Content Visibility

The following information is visible to other HUSH members when you share it:

  • Your work name, username, and role
  • Your city and state
  • Live check-ins (venue, vibe, and photo — only when you choose to go live)
  • Community posts and replies you publish
  • Profile and venue photos (only to mutual followers, unless you change your privacy settings)

Direct messages are private between you and the recipient only.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Notification preferences: You can turn off any notification type in Profile then Notifications at any time.
  • Ghost Mode: Enable Ghost Mode in Profile then Privacy to hide your live check-ins from the community feed.
  • Profile visibility: You can hide your profile photos or venue photos from your privacy settings.
  • Data access or deletion: To request a copy of your data or to have your account permanently deleted, email us at gethush.app@gmail.com or use the Delete Account option in your profile settings.

8. Children Privacy

HUSH is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately at gethush.app@gmail.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the Last updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of HUSH after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact us at: gethush.app@gmail.com
